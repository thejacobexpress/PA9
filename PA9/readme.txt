How to Play:
1. Left click anywhere on the lake to cast your bobber. - Transparent bobber means the rod is on cooldown
2. Wait for a fish to catch.
3. When the "Wrangle" window pops up, the user must keep the fish in the brown rectangle using the space bar to move the rectangle up.
4. When the blue bar on the right of the "Wrangle" window fills completely, you have caught that fish.
5. Press the "M" button to open the market and buy upgrades.
